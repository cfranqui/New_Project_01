Welcome to Ridgewood, Queens.

Ridgewood is a neighborhood located in the New York City borough of Queens. A borough in lay mans terms is basically a collection of neighborhoods which when brought together form a larger neightborhood. If this doesn't make sense its fine, since we will talk about it later. The borough of Queens is in turn part of New York City. Ridgewood is straddled between the Queens-Brooklyn boundary.

Ridgewood was settled by the British in the 17th century as relatively small outpost. A steady stream of German immigrants to Ridgewood in the early 20th century helped to transform Ridgewood from scarcely populated neighborhood to the powerhouse neighborhood it is today.

Things to see

Topos Bookstore, a book store in located on 788 Woodward Ave brings is a unique bookstore that brings a taste "The Shop Around the Corner" to Ridgewood. You can grab a cup of coffee and enjoy a nice book at Topos.

Nowadays Bar No trip to Ridgewood is complete without a visit to Nowadays Bar. Enjoy beer from Queens Brewery alongside sangria and traditional burgers and vegetarian options. If you are visiting from out of town you will enjoy probably the greenest open space in NYC.
